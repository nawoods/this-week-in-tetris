Intro

We're always looking for new contributors, so if you're interested, join our Discord server and say hello. Huge thank you to sonic, who wrote two of the features and provided the Challengers scores for the month. The server also features the Almost-Daily Update, which provides the scores of yesterday's big matches and lets you know what's going on in the future.

The numbers next to the players are their November Premiere Poll rankings, not their tournament seeds. Scores in the tables are given in thousands; an (i) or (a) next to a score indicates that the topout was intentional or aggressive, respectively. As always, let me know if you found this entertaining or useful, or if there's anything else you'd like to see.
