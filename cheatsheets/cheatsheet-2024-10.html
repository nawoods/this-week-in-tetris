This post includes, to the best of my knowledge, all open-playstyle Classic Tetris matches in October that may be relevant to Premiere Poll panelists, with links to the Twitch VODs and This Week in Tetris writeups, if they exist. Remember that the final scoreline may not tell the full story. Numbers next to names refer to October's Premiere Poll rankings.

Since the Premiere Poll reflects matches from the previous three months, you may wish to review the September cheat sheet.

For daily updates, and to be informed when new TWiT posts are released, be sure to join our Discord server. There are bound to be a few errors on this list, so if you notice any, please let me know.
