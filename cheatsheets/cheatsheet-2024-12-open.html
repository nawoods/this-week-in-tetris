This post includes, to the best of our knowledge, all open-playstyle Classic Tetris matches in November that may be relevant to Premiere Poll panelists, with links to the Twitch VODs if they exist. Remember that the final scoreline may not tell the full story. Numbers next to names refer to December's Premiere Poll rankings.

Since the Premiere Poll reflects matches from the previous three months, you may wish to review the November and December cheat sheets.

For daily updates, and to be informed when new TWiT posts are released, be sure to join our Discord server. There are bound to be a few errors on this list, so if you notice any, please let me know.
